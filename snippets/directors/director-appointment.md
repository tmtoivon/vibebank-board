### Directors' Duties

The {{DECISION_MAKER}} noted that directors have the following key duties under Finnish law:

1. **Duty of care** (OYL 1:8, 6:2): Act with the care and diligence of a prudent businessperson
2. **Duty of loyalty**: Act in the company's best interests, not personal interests
3. **Duty to avoid conflicts** (OYL 6:16): Disclose conflicts and abstain from conflicted decisions
4. **Duty to supervise** (OYL 6:2): Oversee management and operations
5. **Liability** (OYL 22:1): Personal liability for damages caused by breach of duties
