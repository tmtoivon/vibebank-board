## Notes

### Legal Limits on Authorisations

**Share issues** (OYL 9:2):
- General Meeting may authorise Board to issue shares
- Maximum duration: 5 years
- Board can issue shares within authorisation limits
- Directed issue with pre-emption waiver: GM must decide (Board cannot even with authorisation)

**Option/convertible issues** (OYL 10:3):
- General Meeting may authorise Board to issue options/convertibles
- Maximum duration: 5 years
- Must specify maximum amount

**Share buybacks** (OYL 15:11):
- General Meeting may authorise Board to acquire own shares
- Maximum: 10% of all shares
- Maximum duration: 18 months
- Must specify price range
- Requires distributable funds
