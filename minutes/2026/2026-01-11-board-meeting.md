#### Vesting Schedule

The options shall vest as follows:

**4-year vesting with 1-year cliff**: 25% (375 options) vest on 11.01.2027, then 1/48 of the total grant (approximately 31.25 options) vests monthly thereafter on the 11th day of each month.

Vesting is subject to the recipient's continued employment with the company.

#### Exercise Terms

- **Exercise price**: EUR 0.50 per share as specified above, determined in accordance with the plan's pricing methodology
- **Exercise period**: Options may be exercised at any time after vesting until 11.01.2036
- **Exercise method**: Written notice to the company with payment of exercise price
- **Payment**: Cash payment

#### Terms on Termination

In the event of termination of employment:

- **Good leaver** (resignation with notice, mutual termination, redundancy): Vested options remain exercisable for 90 days following termination date; all unvested options forfeit immediately.
- **Bad leaver** (termination for cause, breach of contract): All options (vested and unvested) forfeit immediately upon termination.
- **Death or disability**: All unvested options vest immediately; estate or recipient has 12 months from date of death/disability to exercise.

#### Shares upon Exercise

Upon exercise, the company shall issue ordinary shares with the following rights:
- Identical voting rights to existing ordinary shares
- Identical economic rights (dividends, liquidation proceeds)
- Dividend rights from date of registration in shareholder register
- Subject to any transfer restrictions in the Articles of Association
- Registration with PRH following issuance
