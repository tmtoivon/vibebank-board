This written resolution is passed without a meeting in accordance with Chapter 6, Section 3 of the Finnish Companies Act (osakeyhtiölaki 624/2006, "OYL 6:3"), which permits the Board of Directors to pass resolutions without a meeting unless prohibited by the company's Articles of Association.
